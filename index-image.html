ABOUT

My name is Quentn G. Wright and I currently reside in Atlanta, GA. I am currently enrolled in an on-line full stack web developer bootcamp hosted by PENN LPS, located in Philadelphia, PA. I enjoy learning everything there is to know in regards to developing from Front End to Back End. I am extremely passionate about making a difference.
